About Our Club


The George Washington Women's Club Lacrosse team is a student organization that allows participants to continue playing lacrosse at the collegiate level. In addition to field practices during the week, we also have workouts, team dinners, fundraisers, social events, and of course, games and tournaments in the DMV. We play during both the Fall and Spring seasons, and hold tryouts at the beginning of both.
